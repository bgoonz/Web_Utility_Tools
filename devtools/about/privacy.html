Privacy Policy

General

We do not sell, rent or share collected information from this website in ways different from what is written here.

RapidTables uses Google Analytics to monitor site usage in order to improve the quality of the pages. Google Analytics use cookies. Google Analytics collects statistical, non-personal data.

Read how Google uses data when you use our partners sites or apps..

See also: Google Analytics cookie usage, Google privacy policy.

Google AdSense

Third party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites.

See: Google Ads Certified External Vendors

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to the users based on their visit to your sites and/or other sites on the Internet.

Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ads Settings and www.aboutads.info or http://optout.networkadvertising.org.

Content delivery

Rapidtables.com uses Cloudflare for website content delivery. Cloudflare may use several cookies to support its functionality. See: Cloudflare Cookies.

Application Data

RapidTables may save user's settings or user's application data in the browser's local storage and not in our server, so next time the user will enter the page, the data will be available to the user. The Application data can be deleted by the user by clearing the input data or by clearing the browser's Cache.

User's Feedback

User's feedback is only used to improve RapidTables.com.

The feedback is stored in Gmail account.

The user's feedback will not be given to any third party and will be deleted after handling.

Social Share Buttons

RapidTables pages may contain social share buttons. When the user presses the share button, it will share information, like URL and other user contents to the social web.

Multimedia Applications

Some RapidTables pages have multimedia applications that use the camera and microphone. The camera and microphone are activated only after the user allowed access. The recorded information may be processed and stored locally and not sent to our server.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may change from time to time.

Contact information

For questions about this privacy policy, you may email to: feedback.rapidtables at gmail.com.
