GST tax calculation

GST amount = GST Rate(%) × Net amount

Gross amount = (100% + GST Rate(%)) × Net amount

Example

Net amount = 100$
GST Rate = 10%

GST amount = 10%×100$ = 10$

Gross amount = (100%+10%)×100% = 110$
