How to convert picocoulombs to coulombs

1C = 1012pC

or

1pC = 10-12C

Picocoulombs to coulombs conversion formula

The charge in coulombs Q(C) is equal to the charge in picocoulombs Q(pC) divided by 1012:

Q(C) = Q(pC) / 1012

Example

Convert 3 picocoulombs to coulombs:

Q(C) = 3pC / 1012 = 3⋅10-12C
