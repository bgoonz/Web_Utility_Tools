How to convert 5 kJ to joules

How to convert energy of 5 kilojoules (kJ) to joules (J).

The energy E in joules (J) is equal to 5 kilojoules times 1000:

E(J) = 5kJ × 1000 = 5000J

 

So 5 kilojoules (kJ) are equal to 5000 joules (J):

5 kJ = 5000 J
