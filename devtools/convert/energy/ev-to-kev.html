How to convert eV to keV

One electron-volt is equal to thousandth of a kiloelectron-volt:

1eV = 0.001keV

The energy in kiloelectron-volt E(keV) is equal to the energy in electron-volt E(eV) divided by 1000:

E(keV) = E(eV) / 1000

 

Example

Convert 500eV to keV:

E(keV) = 500eV / 1000 = 0.5keV
