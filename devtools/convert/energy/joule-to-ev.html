How to convert joules to eV

One joule is equal to 6.241509⋅1018 electron-volts:

1J = 6.241509e18 eV = 6.241509⋅1018 eV

 

So the energy in electron-volts E(eV) is equal to the energy in joules E(J) times 6.241509⋅1018:

E(eV) = E(J) × 6.241509⋅1018
