How to convert Kilojoules to BTU

1 kJ = 0.94781712 BTU(IT)

1 BTU(IT) = 1.05505585262 kJ

The energy E(BTU) in BTUs is equal to 0.94781712 times the energy E(kJ) in kilojoules (kJ):

E(BTU) = E(kJ) / 1.05505585262 = 0.94781712 ⋅ E(kJ)

 

For example: when E=0.5kJ,

E(BTU) = 0.5kJ / 1.05505585262 = 0.473909 BTU
