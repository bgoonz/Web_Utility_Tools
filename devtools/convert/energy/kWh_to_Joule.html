How to convert kWh to joules

One kilowatt-hour is equal to 3600000 joules:

1kWh = 3600000J = 3.6⋅106J = 3.6MJ

 

So the energy in joules E(J) is equal to 3600000 times the energy in kilowatt-hour E(kWh):

E(J) = 3600000 ⋅ E(kWh)
