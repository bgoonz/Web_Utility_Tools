How to convert MeV to eV

One megaelectron-volt is equal to 1000000 electron-volts:

1MeV = 1000000eV

The energy in electron-volts E(eV) is equal to the energy in megaelectron-volts E(MeV) times 1000000:

E(eV) = E(MeV) × 1000000

 

Example

Convert 5MeV to eV:

E(eV) = 5MeV × 1000000 = 5000000eV
