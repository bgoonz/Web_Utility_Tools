1 gigahertz to hertz conversion

How to convert 1 gigahertz (GHz) to hertz (Hz).

Calculation

The frequency f in hertz (Hz) is equal to 1 gigahertz (GHz) times 109:

f(Hz) = 1 GHz × 109 = 109 Hz

 

So 1 gigahertz is equal to to 109 hertz:

1 GHz = 1000000000 Hz = 109 Hz
