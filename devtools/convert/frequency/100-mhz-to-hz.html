100 megahertz to hertz conversion

How to convert 100 megahertz (MHz) to hertz (Hz).

Calculation

The frequency f in hertz (Hz) is equal to 100 megahertz (MHz) times 1000000:

f(Hz) = 100 MHz × 1000000 = 108 Hz

 

So 100 megahertz is equal to to 108 hertz:

100 MHz = 100000000 Hz = 108 Hz
