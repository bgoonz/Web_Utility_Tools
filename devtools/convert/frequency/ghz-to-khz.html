How to convert gigahertz to kilohertz

1GHz = 1000000kHz

or

1kHz = 0.000001GHz

Gigahertz to kilohertz formula

The frequency f in kilohertz (kHz) is equal to the frequency f in gigahertz (GHz) times 1000000:

f(kHz) = f(GHz) × 1000000

Example

Convert 3 gigahertz to kilohertz:

f(kHz) = 3GHz × 1000000 = 3000000kHz
