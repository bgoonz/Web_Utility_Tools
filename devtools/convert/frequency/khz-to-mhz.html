How to convert kilohertz to megahertz

1kHz = 0.001MHz

or

1MHz = 1000kHz

Kilohertz to megahertz formula

The frequency f in hertz (Hz) is equal to the frequency f in kilohertz (kHz) divided by 1000:

f(MHz) = f(kHz) / 1000

Example

Convert 3 kilohertz to megahertz:

f(MHz) = 3kHz / 1000 = 0.003MHz
