How to convert megahertz to hertz

1Hz = 0.000001MHz

or

1MHz = 1000000Hz

Megahertz to hertz formula

The frequency f in hertz (Hz) is equal to the frequency f in megahertz (MHz) times 1000000:

f(Hz) = f(MHz) × 1000000

Example

Convert 3 megahertz to hertz:

f(Hz) = 3MHz × 1000000 = 3000000Hz
