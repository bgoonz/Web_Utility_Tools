How to calculate hertz from rad/sec

1 Hz = 2π rad/s = 6.2831853 rad/s

or

1 rad/s = 1/2π Hz = 0.1591549 Hz

Rad/s to hertz formula

the frequency f in hertz (Hz) is equal to the angular frequency or angular velocity ω in radians per second (rad/s) divided by 2π:

f(Hz) = ω(rad/s) / 2π

Example

Calculate frequency in hertz from angular velocity of 300 rad/s:

f(Hz) = 300rad/s / 2π = 47.75 Hz
