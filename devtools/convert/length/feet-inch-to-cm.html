How to convert feet+inches to centimeters

1 foot is equal to 30.48 centimeters:

1 ft = 30.48 cm

1 inch is equal to 2.54 centimeters:

1 in = 2.54 cm

The distance d in centimeters (cm) is equal to the distance d in feet (ft) times 30.48 plus the distance d in inches (in) times 2.54:

d(cm) = d(ft) × 30.48 + d(in) × 2.54

Example

Convert 2 feet + 3 inches to centimeters:

d(cm) = 2ft × 30.48 + 3in × 2.54 = 68.58cm
