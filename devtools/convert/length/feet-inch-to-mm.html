How to convert feet+inches to millimeters

1 foot is equal to 304.8 millimeters:

1 ft = 304.8 mm

1 inch is equal to 25.4 millimeters:

1 in = 25.4 mm

The distance d in millimeters (mm) is equal to the distance d in feet (ft) times 30.48 plus the distance d in inches (in) times 25.4:

d(mm) = d(ft) × 304.8 + d(in) × 25.4

Example

Convert 2 feet + 3 inches to millimeters:

d(mm) = 2ft × 304.8 + 3in × 25.4 = 685.8mm
