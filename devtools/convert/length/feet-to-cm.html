How to convert feet to centimeters

1 feet is equal to 30.48 centimeters:

1ft = 30.48cm

The distance d in centimeters (cm) is equal to the distance d in feet (ft) times 30.48:

d(cm) = d(ft) × 30.48

Example

Convert 20 feet to centimeters:

d(cm) = 20ft × 30.48 = 609.6cm

How many feet in a centimeter

One centimeter is equal to 0.032808 feet:

1cm = 1cm / 30.48cm/ft = 0.032808ft

How many centimeters in a foot

One foot is equal to 30.48 centimeters:

1ft = 30.48×1ft = 30.48cm

How to convert 10 feet to centimeterss

Multiply 10 feet by 30.48 to get centimeters:

10ft = 30.48×10ft = 304.8cm
