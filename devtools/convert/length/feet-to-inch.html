How to convert feet to inches

1 foot is equal to 12 inches:

1ft = 12″

The distance d in inches (″) is equal to the distance d in feet (ft) times 12:

d(″) = d(ft) × 12

Example

Convert 2 feet to inches:

d(″) = 2ft × 12 = 24″
