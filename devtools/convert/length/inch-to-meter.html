How to convert inches to meters

1 inch is equal to 0.0254 meters:

1″ = 0.0254m

The distance d in meters (m) is equal to the distance d in inches (″) times 0.0254:

d(m) = d(″) × 0.0254

Example

Convert 20 inches to meters:

d(m) = 20″ × 0.0254 = 0.508m
