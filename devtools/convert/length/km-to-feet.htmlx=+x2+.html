How to convert kilometers to feet

1 kilometer is equal to 3280.84 feet:

1km = 3280.84ft

The distance d in feet (ft) is equal to the distance d in kilometers (km) times 3280.84:

d(ft) = d(km) × 3280.84

Example

Convert 20 kilometers to feet:

d(ft) = 20km × 3280.84 = 65616.8ft
