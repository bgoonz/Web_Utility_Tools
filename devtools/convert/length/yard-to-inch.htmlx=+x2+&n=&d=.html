How to convert yards to inches

1 yard is equal to 3 inches:

1yd = 36in

The distance d in inches (in) is equal to the distance d in yards (yd) times 36:

d(in) = d(yd) × 36

Example

Convert 20 yards to inches:

d(in) = 20yd × 36 = 720in

How many yards in an inch

One inch is equal to 1/36 yards:

1in = 1in / 36 = 0.02777777778yd

How many inches in a yards

One yard is equal to 36 inches:

1yd = 36×1yd = 36in

How to convert 10 yards to inches

Multiply 10 yards by 36 to get inches:

10yd = 36×10yd = 360in
