How to convert yards to meters

1 yard is equal to 0.9144 meters:

1yd = 0.9144m

The distance d in meters (m) is equal to the distance d in yards (yd) times 0.9144:

d(m) = d(yd) × 0.9144

Example

Convert 20 yards to meters:

d(m) = 20yd × 0.9144 = 18.288m

How many yards in a meter

One meter is equal to 1.0936 yards:

1m = 1m / (0.9144m/yd) = 1.0936yd

How many meters in a yards

One yard is equal to 0.9144 meters:

1yd = 0.9144×1yd = 0.9144m

How to convert 10 yards to meters

Multiply 10 yards by 0.9144 to get meters:

10yd = 0.9144×10yd = 9.144m
