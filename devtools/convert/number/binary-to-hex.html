How to convert binary to hex

Convert every 4 binary digits (start from bit 0) to 1 hex digit, with this table:

Binary Hex
0000 0
0001 1
0010 2
0011 3
0100 4
0101 5
0110 6
0111 7
1000 8
1001 9
1010 A
1011 B
1100 C
1101 D
1110 E
1111 F
