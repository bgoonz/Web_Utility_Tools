How to convert decimal to hex

Conversion steps:

  1. Divide the number by 16.
  2. Get the integer quotient for the next iteration.
  3. Get the remainder for the hex digit.
  4. Repeat the steps until the quotient is equal to 0.

Example #1

Convert 756210 to hex:

Division
by 16		 Quotient Remainder
(decimal)		 Remainder
(hex)		 Digit #
7562/16 472 10 A 0
472/16 29 8 8 1
29/16 1 13 D 2
1/16 0 1 1 3

So 756210 = 1D8A16

Example #2

Convert 3563110 to hex:

Division
by 16		 Quotient Remainder
(decimal)		 Remainder
(hex)		 Digit #
35631/16 2226 15 F 0
2226/16 139 2 2 1
139/16 8 12 B 2
8/16 0 8 8 3

So 3563110 = 8B2F16
