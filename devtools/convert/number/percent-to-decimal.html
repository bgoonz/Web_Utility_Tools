How to convert percent to decimal

In order to convert percent to decimal number, the percentage should be divided by 100:

1% = 1 / 100 = 0.01

5% = 5/100 = 0.05

10% = 10/100 = 0.1

35% = 35/100 = 0.35

50% = 50/100 = 0.5

100% = 100/100 = 1

230% = 230/100 = 2.3
