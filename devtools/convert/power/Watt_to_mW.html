How to convert watts to milliwatts

One watt is equal to 1000 milliwatts:

1W = 1000mW

Watts to milliwatts conversion formula

The power in milliwatts P(mW) is equal to 1000 times the power in watts P(W):

P(mW) = 1000 ⋅ P(W)

Example

Convert 5W to milliwatts:

P(mW) = 1000 ⋅ 5W = 5000mW
