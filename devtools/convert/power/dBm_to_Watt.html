How to convert dBm to watts

The power conversion of dBm to watts is given by the formula:

P(W) = 1W ⋅ 10(P(dBm) / 10) / 1000 = 10((P(dBm)- 30) / 10)

Example

Convert 43dBm to watts:

P(W) = 1W ⋅ 10(43dBm/ 10) / 1000 = 19.9526W
