How to convert gigawatts to watts

1GW = 109W = 1000000000 W

1W = 10-9GW = 1/1000000000 GW

Gigawatts to watts conversion formula

The power in watts P(W) is equal to 109 times the power in gigawatts P(GW):

P(W) = 109 × P(GW)

Example

Convert 3GW to watts:

P(W) = 109 × 3GW = 3×109W = 3000000000W
