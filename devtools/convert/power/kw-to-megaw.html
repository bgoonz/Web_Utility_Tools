How to convert kilowatts to megawatts

1MW = 1000kW

1kW = 0.001MW

Kilowatts to megawatts conversion formula

The power in megawatts P(MW) is equal to the power in kilowatts P(kW) divided by 1000:

P(MW) = P(kW) / 1000

Example

Convert 5kW to megawatts:

P(MW) = 5kW / 1000 = 0.005MW
