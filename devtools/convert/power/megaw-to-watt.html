How to convert megawatts to watts

1MW = 1000000W

1W = 0.000001MW

Megawatts to watts conversion formula

The power in watts P(W) is equal to 1000000 times the power in megawatts P(MW):

P(W) = 1000000 × P(MW)

Example

Convert 3MW to watts:

P(W) = 1000000 × 3MW = 3000000W
