How to convert watts to gigawatts

1W = 10-9GW = 1/1000000000 GW

1GW = 109W = 1000000000 W

Watts to gigawatts conversion formula

The power in gigawatts P(GW) is equal to the power in watts P(W) divided by 109:

P(GW) = P(W) / 109

Example

Convert 5W to gigawatts:

P(GW) = 5W / 109 = 5×10-9GW
