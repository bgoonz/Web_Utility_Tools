425 Fahrenheit to Celsius conversion

How to convert 425 degrees Fahrenheit to Celsius.

The temperature T in degrees Celsius (°C) is equal to the temperature T in degrees Fahrenheit (°F) minus 32, times 5/9:

T(°C) = (T(°F) - 32) × 5/9 = (425°F - 32) × 5/9 = 218.333°C

So 425 degrees Fahrenheit are equal to 218.333 degrees Celsius:

425°F = 218.333°C
