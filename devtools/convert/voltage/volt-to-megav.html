How to convet volts to megavolts

1 V = 10-6 MV = 0.000001 MV

or

1 MV = 106 V = 1000000 V

Volts to megavolts formula

The voltage V in megavolts (MV) is equal to the voltage V in volts (V) divided by 1000:

V(MV) = V(V) / 1000

Example

Convert 3 volts to megavolts:

V(MV) = 3V / 1000000 = 0.000003 MV
