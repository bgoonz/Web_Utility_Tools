How to convet volts to millivolts

1 V = 103 mV = 1000 mV

or

1 mV = 10-3 V = 0.001 V

Volts to millivolts formula

The voltage V in millivolts (mV) is equal to the voltage V in volts (V) times 1000:

V(mV) = V(V) × 1000

Example

Convert 3 volts to millivolts:

V(mV) = 3V × 1000 = 3000 mV
