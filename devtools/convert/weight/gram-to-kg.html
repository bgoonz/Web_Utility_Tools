How to convert Grams to Kilograms

1 gram (g) is equal to 0.001 kilograms (kg).

1 g = (1/1000) kg = 0.001 kg

The mass m in kilograms (kg) is equal to the mass m in grams (g) divided by 1000:

m(kg) = m(g) / 1000

Example

Convert 5 g to kilograms:

m(kg) = 5 g / 1000 = 0.005 kg
