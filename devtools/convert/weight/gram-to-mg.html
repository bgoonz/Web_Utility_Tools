How to convert Grams to Milligrams

1 gram (g) is equal to 1000 milligrams (mg).

1 g = 1000 mg

The mass m in milligrams (mg) is equal to the mass m in grams (g) times 1000:

m(mg) = m(g) × 1000

Example

Convert 5 g to milligrams:

m(mg) = 5 g × 1000 = 5000 mg
