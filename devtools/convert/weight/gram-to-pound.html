How to convert Grams to Pounds

1 gram (g) is equal to 0.00220462262185 pounds (lbs).

1 g = 0.00220462262185 lb

The mass m in pounds (lb) is equal to the mass m in grams (g) divided by 453.59237:

m(lb) = m(g) / 453.59237

Example

Convert 5g to pounds:

m(lb) = 5 g / 453.59237 = 0.011023 lb
