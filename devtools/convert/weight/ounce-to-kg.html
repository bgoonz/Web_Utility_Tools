How to convert Ounces to Kilograms

1 ounce (oz) is equal to 0.02834952 kilograms (kg).

1 oz = 0.02834952 kg

The mass m in kilograms (kg) is equal to the mass m in ounces (oz) times 0.02834952:

m(kg) = m(oz) × 0.02834952

Example

Convert 5 oz to kilograms:

m(kg) = 5 oz × 0.028.34952 = 0.1417476 kg
