How to convert Pounds to Ounces

1 pound (lb) is equal to 16 Ounces (oz).

1 lb = 16 oz

The mass m in ounces (oz) is equal to the mass m in pounds (lb) times 16:

m(oz) = m(lb) × 16

Example

Convert 5 lb to ounces:

m(oz) = 5 lb × 16 = 80 oz
