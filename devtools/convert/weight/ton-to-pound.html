How to convert Tons to Pounds

1 ton (t) is equal to 2204.62262185 pounds (lbs).

1 t = 2204.62262185 lb

The mass m in pounds (lb) is equal to the mass m in tons (t) divided by 0.00045359237:

m(lb) = m(t) / 0.00045359237

Example

Convert 5t to pounds:

m(lb) = 5 t / 0.00045359237 = 11023 lb
