XXXIX Roman Numeral

What is the XXXIX roman numeral equal to?

The XXXIX roman numeral is equal to the number 39:

XXXIX = X+X+X+(X-I) = 10+10+10+(10-1) = 39

The X roman numeral is equal to the number 10:

X = 10

IX is equal to X minus I:

IX = X-I = 10-1 = 9

So the roman numeral XXXIX is equal to 39:

XXXIX = 39
