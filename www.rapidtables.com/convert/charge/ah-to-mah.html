How to convert ampere-hours to milliampere-hours

1mAh = 0.001Ah

or

1Ah = 1000mAh

Ampere-hours to milliampere-hours formula

The charge in milliampere-hours Q(mAh) is equal to the charge in ampere-hours Q(Ah) times 1000:

Q(mAh) = Q(Ah) × 1000

Example

Convert 3 ampere-hours to milliampere-hours:

Q(mAh) = 3Ah × 1000 = 3000mAh
