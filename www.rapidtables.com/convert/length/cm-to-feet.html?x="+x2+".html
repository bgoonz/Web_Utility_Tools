How to convert centimeters to feet

1 centimeter is equal to 0.3937007874 feet:

1cm = (1/2.54)inch = 0.3937007874ft

The distance d in feet (ft) is equal to the distance d in centimeters (cm) divided by 30.48:

d(ft) = d(cm) / 30.48

Example

Convert 20 cm to feet:

d(ft) = 20cm / 30.48 = 0.656168ft

How many feet in a centimeter

One centimeter is equal to 0.0328084 feet:

1cm = 1cm / 30.48cm/ft = 0.0328084ft

How many centimeters in a foot

One foot is equal to 30.48 centimeters:

1ft = 30.48×1ft = 30.48cm

How to convert 10cm to feet

Divide 10 centimeters by 30.48 to get feet:

10cm = 10cm / 30.48cm/ft = 0.328084ft
