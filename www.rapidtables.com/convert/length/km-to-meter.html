How to convert kilometers to meters

1 kilometer is equal to 1000 meters:

1km = 1000m

The distance d in meters (m) is equal to the distance d in kilometers (km) times 1000:

d(m) = d(km) × 1000

Example

Convert 20 kilometers to meters:

d(m) = 20km × 1000 = 20000m

How many meters in a kilometer

One kilometer is equal to 1000 meter:

1km = 1km×1000 = 1000m

How many kilometers in a meter

One meter is equal to 0.001 kilometers:

1m = 1m/1000 = 0.001km

How to convert 8 kilometers to meters

Multiply 8 kilometers by 1000 to get meters:

8km = 1000×8km = 8000m
