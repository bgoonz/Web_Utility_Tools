How to convert kilowatts to watts

1kW = 1000W

Kilowatts to watts conversion formula

The power in watts P(W) is equal to 1000 times the power in kilowatts P(kW):

P(W) = 1000 ⋅ P(kW)

Example

Convert 1.3kW to watts:

P(W) = 1000 ⋅ 1.3kW = 1300W
