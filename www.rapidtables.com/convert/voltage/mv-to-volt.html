How to convet millivolts to volts

1 V = 103 mV = 1000 mV

or

1 mV = 10-3 V = 0.001 V

Millivolts to volts formula

The voltage V in millivolts (mV) is equal to the voltage V in volts (V) divided by 1000:

V(V) = V(mV) / 1000

Example

Convert 3 millivolts to volts:

V(V) = 3mV / 1000 = 0.003 V
